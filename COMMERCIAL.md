# Commercial Licensing

This project is made publicly available under a **non-commercial license** for personal, academic, research, and non-profit use.

Any **commercial use** requires a separate commercial license from the copyright holder.

## What Counts as Commercial Use

Commercial use includes, but is not limited to:

* Use in products or systems that are sold or licensed for a fee
* Integration into commercial hardware or software products
* Use as part of a paid service, SaaS, or subscription offering
* Use in internal commercial R&D for product development
* Use by for-profit entities beyond evaluation or prototyping
* Consulting, contracting, or service delivery based on this software

If you are unsure whether your use is commercial, assume it is and contact me.

## Commercial Licensing Options

Commercial licenses are offered on a case-by-case basis and may include:

### 1. Commercial Evaluation License

For companies wishing to:

* evaluate the software in a commercial context
* perform feasibility studies or proof-of-concept work

Typically:

* time-limited
* non-production use
* low or no cost

### 2. Production License

For:

* shipping products
* commercial deployments
* operational use

Terms may vary based on:

* deployment scale
* safety-criticality
* field of use
* support requirements

### 3. OEM / Embedded License

For:

* hardware integration
* resale or redistribution as part of a product

Typically includes:

* redistribution rights
* warranty disclaimers
* indemnification clauses
